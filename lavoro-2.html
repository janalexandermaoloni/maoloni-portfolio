Fieldnote

Product design and front-end for FieldNote AI, an app that turns voice notes and site photos into finished field reports for structural engineers. Less paperwork, more field.

Client
FieldNote AI
Role
Product design & front-end
Year
2025
Type
Web & mobile app

The project

Site inspections generate a mountain of notes, photos and measurements, and then hours of desk work turning them into a formal report. FieldNote AI set out to collapse that second step: capture on site by voice and camera, and let the app assemble a structured, professional report.

The client

FieldNote AI is a focused team building software for structural and civil engineers. They needed a product interface that felt trustworthy to professionals, worked with gloves-on in the field, and stayed calm despite handling a lot of data.

Technologies

  • Front-end built in clean, semantic HTML, CSS and JavaScript.
  • A component-driven UI: capture flow, annotated photos, report preview.
  • Accessible forms, large touch targets, offline-friendly patterns.
  • Design tokens shared between marketing site and product for consistency.

Creative process

I shadowed the real workflow first, what happens on site, what breaks back at the office. That shaped a capture-first interface: voice and photo front and centre, report generation one tap away. Clickable prototypes went straight into production-quality front-end, tightening the loop between design intent and shipped screens.

Results

The result is an app that respects an engineer's time: capture in the field, review a draft in minutes, export a clean report. A calm, confident interface for a genuinely tedious job, now a lot less tedious.
